A fork of freeCodeCamp

The intent of this fork is to provide an interface to the freeCodeCamp curriculum that:

  • allows you to pick from a variety of both light and dark themes
  • allows you to pick from a variety of both light and dark editor themes
  • allows you to login with Github instead of giving out your email for passwordless authentication
  • ensures your privacy by performing zero analytics on your activities on the site
  • does not popup a donate window over and over and over

Why you might want to use this version

  • you want wider variety of theme options
  • you dislike passwordless authentication
  • you are sick of the "donate" popup
  • you want greater control of the lesson layout

Why you might NOT want to use this version

  • you want the "official" freeCodeCamp certifications
  • you want to contribute to the original, rather than a fork
  • you really like the UI that's currently available to you on freeCodeCamp
